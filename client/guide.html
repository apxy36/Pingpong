Frog Pong: The Game

Overview

In an alternate universe, the game of ping pong has been (nearly completely) reinvented.

The game is now played with frogs and towers, and the objective is for you to toggle them quick enough to whittle down the opponent's base.

(Yes, this is quite a revamp)

Rules

The rules are simple:

  • There are two teams in the game, with 2 players each.
  • Each team has a base with 100 health points.
  • The objective is to toggle the frogs and towers to attack the opponent's base. each game lasts 3 minutes.
  • The first player to reduce the opponent's base to 0 health points wins.
  • If no team is able to deplete their opponent's base when the timer ends, the team whose base has the highest HP will win.
  • The game will end in a draw if both bases have the same HP.

Towers

Towers are stationary units that can have a range of effects.

There are 5 types of towers, corresponding to the types of frogs on them.

Normal Tower

Deals 15 damage to the opponent's base.

Speed Tower

Increases the movement speed of your team's players for 20s.

Ice Tower

Slows the movement speed of opponent players for 20s.

Buff Tower

Increases the damage dealt by normal towers (by your team) by 5.

Healing Tower

Heals your base for 10 HP.

How to Play

Use WASD to move around, and the SPACE bar to toggle the towers.

You can only toggle towers when you are close to them.

Notice the yellow ring.
